Become the Account Wesley Chapel Parents Actually Follow

Presented by John Dalton & Ohm Patel · June 2026
The Idea
Most school social media pages post about the school. Parents don't follow accounts that only talk about themselves. They follow accounts that make their lives easier — that tell them what's happening in their neighborhood, where to take their kids, what to know this week.

The play is simple: make the Goddard Wesley Chapel account the most useful thing a local parent follows. Not just a school page — a local resource. That trust is what turns followers into enrollment inquiries.
There's proof this works. Only in Naples 239 built a 500K+ following doing exactly this for Naples, FL — local news, community content, business collabs. They're not a school. But the model is the same: become the account people check because it tells them something useful about where they live.
